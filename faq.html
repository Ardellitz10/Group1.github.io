FAQ on FAQ
Purpose?

To provide a step-by-step process for creating future FAQ and Procedure entries in a consistent and easy-to-follow format.

Step 1: Identify the topic

decide whether the entry is an FAQ or a procedure and keep the scope focused on one main question and process.

Step 2: Use the standard format

title/question, purpose, steps/answer, and tips/notes in that order

Step 3: Keep it clear and consistent

use short sentences and simple language, for procedures list steps in order of which they are done, for FAQs start with direct answer and expand if needed.

Step 4: Review before publishing

check that entry is easy to understand and make sure format is consistent.
